Introduction

During the development of this self-assessment, three types of information requirements will be identified::

  • Those that require a yes or no answer (YES - NO)
  • Those that request reporting quantities or listing areas, specialties, etc.
  • Those that contain a premise or statement that must be evaluated based on its degree of compliance or acceptance within the organization.

The accuracy of the provided responses and the honest assessment of the statements' compliance will allow for a clear understanding of the organization's situation in facing the challenge of efficient business risk management.

The following factors are evaluated::

  1. Corporate Governance Risk Management
  2. Risk Appetite Management
  3. Market Risk Management
  4. Process Risk Management
  5. Financial Situation
  6. Results Generation

All situations presented require the selection of at least one answer, ensuring a comprehensive and accurate evaluation of each area.

This process can be completed in stages, allowing for the partial completion of the required items and the ability to continue later from the last reported point.

The application enables access to each section once the previous section has been completed.

Upon completion of all factors, a general summary with the score obtained is presented, providing an overall view of the company's position in facing the challenge of risk management.
